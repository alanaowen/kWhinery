Services

Something snazzy here...

Full Design

A complete tailor-made design for your home. Paragraph or bullets detailing what this will consist of; Consultation, discussing style, color preferences, budget, and scope of project, CAD drwaings, mood/vision boards, material boards, shopping, transport and install furniture, etc...

Room Styling

Style a space to create a look and atmosphere for a special occasion or for a more permanent impact. Paragraph or bullets detailing whats offered; Dress a space for a special celebration or event, bring interior up to date with the latest trends, seasonal changes, etc...

Quick Fix

Maybe this could be an online only offering to help clients re-love their space using new or existing room furnishings and accessories. You could offer digital mood boards, hints, and a shopping list.
